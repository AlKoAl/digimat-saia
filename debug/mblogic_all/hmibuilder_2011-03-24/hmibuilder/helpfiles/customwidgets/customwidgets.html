Creating Custom Widgets

Overview

As well as using the provided SVG HMI widgets, you can create your own custom widgets and add them to your library. Creating a custom widget is the easiest way to add a special graphic to your HMI.

This section will discuss what widgets are, how they work, and how to create your own.

  • Widget Basics - The basic types and characteristics of SVG HMI widgets.
  • Passive Widget - A simple passive widget.
  • HMIBuilder Data - The additional data used by HMIBuilder.
  • Active Widgets - Creating active widgets.
